## 📏 Qualitäts-Standards

### UI/UX (Perfektion bis ins Detail)
- ✅ **Typography:** Distinctive Fonts (kein Inter/Roboto!)
- ✅ **Colors:** Atmosphärische Paletten, konsistent
- ✅ **Layout:** Unexpected compositions, spatial design
- ✅ **Components:** Button, Card, Input, Badge mit Hover-States
- ✅ **Micro-Interactions:** Animations, Transitions überall
- ✅ **Responsive:** Mobile-First, alle Breakpoints
- ✅ **Accessibility:** ARIA, Keyboard-Navigation
- ✅ **Performance:** Lighthouse 90+

### Funktionalität (Perfektion)
- ✅ **TypeScript:** Vollständig type-safe
- ✅ **Tests:** Unit, Integration, E2E
- ✅ **Error-Handling:** User-friendly Messages
- ✅ **Loading-States:** Skeletons, Spinners
- ✅ **Offline-Support:** PWA, Service Worker
- ✅ **Security:** Input-Validation, XSS-Schutz
- ✅ **Documentation:** README, Comments, API-Docs
